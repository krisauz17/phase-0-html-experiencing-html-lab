Kellye's Grilled Chees Sandwich

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 4 slicess of cheese
    • Cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  1. spread butter on bread & pan
  2. place bread in frying pan & fry
  3. add cheese on top of bread
  4. cover with second slide of bread
  5. turn over &fry for 2 minutes
